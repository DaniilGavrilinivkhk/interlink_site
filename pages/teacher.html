Jari Lutta (English language)

Studied at the language gymnasium with English as a special subject and then at the State University of St.Petersburg (Faculty of Philology). Since the mid-1990s worked as a translator/ interpreter, editor, travel guide, and English language teacher. Lived and worked in several countries of the world, using English as the main language. Teaching experience: 20+ years.
Since 2018, Jari has been working as a teacher of English at the Language school ‘InterLink’. Teaches adults and children in groups and individually at different levels (A1 – B2+). Using the modern British coursebooks and audio/ video materials in a friendly classroom atmosphere of our school, students can increase their vocabulary, improve grammar, speaking skills and pronunciation, learn to understand texts and spoken language.
Apart from the general English language course, Jari also teaches ‘English for Business’, ‘English for Logistics’, and the Russian language for foreigners (with English as the medium of instruction).
Participates in seminars and conferences for teachers of English in Estonia.
